1. Nathaniel Greene

2. East Commonlands

3. Near the ancient stone circle atop the hill

Nathaniel Greene, a renowned strategist and tactician, has come to the East Commonlands to study the ancient stone circle believed to possess mystical properties that could enhance strategic acumen. The stories of this place being a gathering spot for druids and sages throughout history have piqued his interest, as he hopes to learn secrets that might aid him in his endeavors. While there, Greene also engages with travelers and merchants, gathering information and trading insights on the art of warfare and diplomacy.
