2. East Freeport

3. Inside the Seafarer's Roost Tavern

Jonathan Greene, a renowned cartographer and sailor, is currently in East Freeport, seeking new opportunities to expand his mapping endeavors. The bustling port city is a hub for travelers and adventurers, making it an ideal place for Greene to gather information on uncharted territories from returning sailors and explorers. Inside the Seafarer's Roost Tavern, he listens intently to tales of distant lands, hoping to add new routes and landmarks to his maps. His presence here is vital as he prepares for an upcoming voyage that promises to unveil the secrets of the untamed seas.
